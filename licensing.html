Licensing

The individual traits are in the public domain (CC0).

Each one of the generated tokens (a unique combination of the traits) and the 1/1 tokens IP commercial rights are given to the token holder.

A token holder can decide to change the license of their token to be in the public domain (CC0) but this is irreversible.
